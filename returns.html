Returns & Refunds Policy

Return Policy Overview

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Our return policy allows you to return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Eligible Items

  • Unused items in original packaging
  • Items with original tags attached
  • Items in original condition
  • Items with all accessories and documentation

Non-Returnable Items

  • Used or damaged items
  • Items without original packaging
  • Personalized or custom-made items
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Gift cards and digital products

Return Process

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Log into your account and go to "Order History"
  2. Select the order containing the item you wish to return
  3. Click "Return Item" and select the reason for return
  4. Print the return label and packing slip
  5. Package the item securely with all original packaging
  6. Attach the return label and drop off at the designated shipping location

Return Shipping

  • Free return shipping for items that arrived damaged or defective
  • Return shipping costs for other returns are the customer's responsibility
  • We recommend using a trackable shipping method
  • Keep your return tracking number for reference

Refund Process

Refund Timeline

  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after receiving the returned item
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Credit card refunds may take 5-10 business days to appear on your statement
  • You will receive an email confirmation when your refund is processed

Refund Amount

  • Full refund of the item price and original shipping cost (if applicable)
  • Return shipping costs are not refunded unless the item was damaged or defective
  • Original shipping costs are not refunded for items returned due to customer preference

Exchanges

Exchange Process

  • Exchanges are available for different sizes, colors, or styles of the same item
  • Follow the same return process and indicate "Exchange" as the reason
  • Include a note specifying the desired replacement item
  • New items will be shipped once the return is received and processed

Exchange Shipping

  • Free shipping for the replacement item
  • Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility
  • Exchanges may take 7-10 business days to process

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact our customer service team within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange for a replacement or refund at no additional cost to you.

Required Information

  • Order number
  • Photos of the damage or defect
  • Description of the issue
  • Preferred resolution (replacement or refund)

International Returns

International returns are subject to the same policy terms, with the following additional considerations:

  • Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility
  • Customs duties and taxes are not refundable
  • Returns must be shipped with a trackable method
  • Processing time may be longer due to customs clearance
