Shipping Policy

Domestic Shipping

Standard Shipping

  • Delivery within 3-5 business days
  • Free shipping on orders over $50
  • Orders under $50: $5.99 shipping fee
  • Processing time: 1-2 business days

Express Shipping

  • Delivery within 1-2 business days
  • Available for all orders
  • Additional $15.99 fee
  • Processing time: Same day (if ordered before 2 PM EST)

International Shipping

Standard International

  • Delivery within 7-14 business days
  • Available to most countries
  • Shipping rates vary by destination
  • Customs and import duties may apply

Express International

  • Delivery within 3-5 business days
  • Available to select countries
  • Additional $25.99 fee
  • Customs and import duties may apply

Order Processing

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Orders placed after 2 PM EST will be processed the next business day. During peak seasons or sales, processing times may be extended.

Order Status Updates

  • Order confirmation email sent immediately after purchase
  • Shipping confirmation email with tracking number when order ships
  • Delivery confirmation email when package is delivered

Shipping Restrictions

Restricted Items

  • Hazardous materials
  • Perishable goods
  • Items requiring special handling
  • Items prohibited by shipping carriers

Restricted Locations

Some items may not be available for shipping to certain locations due to local regulations or carrier restrictions. This will be indicated on the product page.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can track your order status through:

  • Your account dashboard
  • The tracking link in your shipping confirmation email
  • Our website's order tracking page

Shipping Delays

While we strive to deliver all orders on time, occasional delays may occur due to:

  • Weather conditions
  • Carrier delays
  • Customs clearance (for international orders)
  • Peak season volume

We will notify you of any significant delays and provide updated delivery estimates.
